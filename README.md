WordPress Developer and Civil Engineer
--------------------------------------

I specialize in managing and developing WordPress websites using page builders such as Elementor, Beaver Builder, and others. I want to help business owners improve their online presence by building websites and handling other aspects of website management, from content management and site optimization to troubleshooting technical issues and performance enhancements. That way, business owners can focus more on running their business while I drive more potential customers to their websites. I have successfully managed over 30 WordPress websites and completed 200+ client requests while working for a private equity firm in the US.

Before becoming a developer, I am a civil engineer with over six years of experience in construction project management. In those years, I developed my skills in project management, problem-solving, and effective communication. I once had the opportunity to introduce a mobile app I made to help project inspectors in their site inspection, and from that on, I became interested in programming and building applications. During the pandemic, I started learning about web development and transitioned into becoming a WordPress developer. Using my analytical mindset and attention to detail from my engineering background, I quickly adapted to this new field.

My background in engineering and experience in project management & web development allow me to bring a unique perspective to my career. I approach website management with the same precision and commitment to quality that I applied in managing complex construction projects. I am a quick learner, continuously enhancing my skills through various courses and self-learning initiatives, and I am known for being tech-savvy, reliable, and easy to work with.

I am eager to leverage my diverse skill set to help businesses build a strong online presence and achieve their goals, ensuring their websites are functional and effective for growing their business.
